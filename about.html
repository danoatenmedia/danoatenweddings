About Dan Oaten

Videographer | Photographer | Storyteller

Hi, I'm Dan, a passionate videographer and photographer with a keen eye for storytelling. While I specialize in capturing the magic of weddings—those once-in-a-lifetime moments filled with love, emotion, and celebration—my work goes far beyond the aisle.

From high-energy sports and dynamic events to creative projects, I bring the same level of dedication to everything I'm privileged to work on. I also work as a live TV cameraman, where precision, timing, and adaptability keep me on my toes.

Whether it's a quiet glance between newlyweds or the roar of a live crowd, I love being part of the creative process—and bringing those visions to life.
