Meet Dan Oaten

Videographer · Photographer · Storyteller

Hey, I'm Dan — a videographer and photographer based in Suffolk. My interest in film and photography started young, studying art photography before filming music videos for my band and other local artists. That path led here: weddings, live TV, sports and events — but weddings are where my heart is.

I describe my style as relaxed and romantic. I love letting the day unfold and capturing candid moments — glances, laughter, the little details — and shaping them into something cinematic and uniquely yours. On your wedding day I'm calm, easygoing, and quietly in the background until you need me.

Whether it's an intimate Suffolk barn or a destination celebration, I bring the same dedication: story-led films and natural photography you'll want to watch and share for decades.
